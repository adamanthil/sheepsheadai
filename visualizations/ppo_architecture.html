1 · Observation dict state

Every decision step, the agent sees a structured dict describing the game from this player's perspective — header scalars (whose turn, picker, partner mode, etc.), the current trick, the player's hand, the blind/bury piles, and the player's persistent 256-d memory from previous decisions.

2 · Encoder CardReasoningEncoder

Turns the dict into a single 256-d feature vector plus 8 per-hand-slot tokens. Every entity (a card, the trick, the memory, the header) is embedded as one 64-d token; a transformer lets all 19 tokens reason about each other; per-bag attention pooling and a GRU compose them into final features.

Welcome

An interactive map of the PPO actor + critic in ppo.py.

This network plays Sheepshead, a 5-player trick-taking card game. At every decision point it sees a structured snapshot of the game and produces a probability over legal actions (pick, partner choice, bury, play).

How to use:

• Hover any block to see its tensor shapes.
• Click a block to read what it does and why.
• Press ▶ Play to animate a forward pass — each stage lights up as data flows.
• Step advances one stage at a time.
